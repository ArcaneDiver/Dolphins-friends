# Dolphin's friends

Just a mod that adds the ability to ride a dolphin and tame it.

## Tame

To tame the dolphin use salmon.


## Inventory
You can open the dolphin's inventory by clicking I (default) and you can insert a sonar


## Sonar 
By pressing U (default) you can scan the near mobs
